Jewelry Store Robbery in Dallas

A jewelry store in North Dallas was robbed yesterday afternoon. The store, Diamond Treasures, is located at 5500 Preston Road. According to police reports, three masked individuals entered the store around 2:30 PM and smashed several display cases.

The suspects fled with an estimated $200,000 worth of jewelry and watches. Witnesses reported seeing the suspects leave in a black SUV heading south on Preston Road.

Police are asking anyone with information to contact the Dallas Police Department.
